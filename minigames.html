This minigame is based on the Suzanne Collins' franchise, called "The Hunger Games". The game is simple. 24 players called tributes battle it out on an arena. The last one standing is the winner.

The Purge

A game of PvP. Based on a movie trilogy, where every night a year, for 12 hours all crime is legal. In our version, the PvP is enabled at night time. During the day, players can look for weapons and food to fight it out at night.
